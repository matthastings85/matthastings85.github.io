About Me

Hello! My name is Matt. I grew up in South Africa but I’ve been living and working in the USA since 2012. After graduating college in 2008, I have worked in several nonprofit contexts in a wide variety of roles, all of which have required high levels of emotional intelligence and general people skills, not to mention self-motivation and organization.

My amazing wife recently started a job in the publishing industry and we relocated to the Chicagoland area this summer. It is my privilege to support her in this career move, but the result is that I am looking for a new vocational opportunity. This, however, does not tell the whole story. I am excited to harness the skills I’ve learned in nonprofit operations in a new context that will hopefully provide even more opportunities for growth and personal development.
